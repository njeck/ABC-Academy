# DABA AI for Data Analysts Final Assessment

**Document Code:** DABA-AI-003  
**Total Marks:** 100  

## Section A — AI foundations: 10 marks

Explain:

- Two valid uses of generative AI in data analysis.
- Two important limitations.
- Why the analyst remains accountable.

## Section B — Prompt engineering: 15 marks

Rewrite a weak analytical request using the DABA prompt structure:

- Role
- Context
- Task
- Data
- Output
- Constraints
- Validation

## Section C — Technical assistance: 20 marks

Use AI to support:

- One Excel formula.
- One SQL query.
- One Power BI DAX measure.

For each, provide:

- Prompt.
- Initial output.
- Test performed.
- Correction made.
- Final approved solution.

## Section D — Insight validation: 20 marks

Use validated ABC Retail Ltd KPI values to generate five findings.

Complete a validation table identifying:

- Verified facts.
- Interpretations.
- Assumptions.
- Unsupported claims.
- Corrections.

## Section E — Responsible AI: 15 marks

Assess a supplied AI response for:

- Hallucination.
- Privacy.
- Bias.
- Calculation errors.
- Overreliance.

Assign likelihood, impact, risk score, treatment, and owner.

## Section F — Capstone report: 20 marks

Submit an AI-assisted management report containing:

- Five findings.
- Three recommendations.
- One operational risk.
- One data limitation.
- A 250-word executive summary.
- Full prompt and validation evidence.

## Grading

- 80–100: Distinction
- 70–79: Merit
- 60–69: Credit
- 50–59: Pass
- Below 50: Fail

## Mandatory failure conditions

The submission may not pass if the learner:

- Uses real confidential data in an unapproved AI tool.
- Submits fabricated evidence or sources.
- Cannot explain the submitted analysis.
- Fails to validate material calculations or claims.
- Conceals prohibited AI use.
