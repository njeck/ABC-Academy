# DABA Beginner Power BI Final Assessment

**Document Code:** DABA-PBI-002  
**Total Marks:** 100  
**Enterprise:** ABC Retail Ltd  

## Section A — Data Preparation: 20 marks

1. Import all required tables. — 5 marks  
2. Apply correct data types. — 5 marks  
3. Clean text, date, and numeric fields. — 5 marks  
4. Rename and organize queries professionally. — 5 marks  

## Section B — Data Modeling: 20 marks

1. Build the required star-schema relationships. — 10 marks  
2. Mark and use the Date dimension correctly. — 4 marks  
3. Hide technical key fields from report view where appropriate. — 3 marks  
4. Use clear table and field names. — 3 marks  

## Section C — DAX: 25 marks

Create and validate:

- Completed Sales
- Completed Orders
- Average Order Value
- Unique Customers
- Gross Profit
- Gross Margin %
- Sales YTD
- Sales MoM %
- Inventory Value
- Products Requiring Reorder
- Total Budget
- Total Expenses
- Budget Variance

Marks are awarded for correctness, formatting, and appropriate use of measures.

## Section D — Dashboard Design: 25 marks

Create three pages:

1. Executive Overview — 10 marks  
2. Sales and Customers — 8 marks  
3. Inventory and Finance — 7 marks  

The dashboard must include:

- KPI cards
- Trends
- Rankings
- Filters
- Clear titles
- Consistent branding
- Readable labels
- Appropriate visual choices

## Section E — Business Interpretation: 10 marks

Provide:

- Five accurate business insights.
- Three practical management recommendations.
- One major operational risk.
- One data-quality limitation.

## Pass Criteria

- 50–59: Pass
- 60–69: Credit
- 70–79: Merit
- 80–100: Distinction

## Instructor Evaluation Notes

The report should not be graded only for visual appearance. Marks must also reflect:

- Correct model design.
- Correct calculations.
- Appropriate business interpretation.
- Accuracy of filters and interactions.
- Professional organization.
