# ABC Retail Ltd Departmental Analytics Casebook

**Document Code:** DABA-ABC-CASE-002  
**Version:** 1.0  

## Purpose

This casebook extends the ABC Retail Ltd enterprise simulation into a structured set of departmental analytical assignments. It enables instructors to teach the same analytical concepts through realistic business situations rather than isolated exercises.

## Departments covered

- Executive Management
- Sales
- Finance
- Human Resources
- Inventory
- Procurement
- Logistics
- Marketing
- Customer Service
- Information Technology
- Information Security
- Risk, Audit, and Business Intelligence

## Case delivery method

Each case should follow this sequence:

1. Introduce the business scenario.
2. Identify the stakeholder and decision required.
3. Confirm the relevant datasets and definitions.
4. Assign the analytical questions.
5. Require the student to document formulas, queries, measures, and assumptions.
6. Validate the results against source data.
7. Produce findings and recommendations.
8. Present the result to a simulated manager.

## Required student evidence

Each submission should include:

- Business question.
- Data source.
- Cleaning or preparation completed.
- Excel formula, SQL query, or DAX measure.
- Result extract.
- Validation check.
- Finding.
- Recommendation.
- Limitation.
- Final presentation or report.

## Recommended assessment weighting

- Business understanding: 10%
- Data preparation: 15%
- Technical analysis: 30%
- Validation and accuracy: 15%
- Findings and recommendations: 15%
- Communication and presentation: 15%

## Instructor control rules

- Do not accept unsupported findings.
- Recalculate at least one KPI independently.
- Ask the learner to explain one formula, one query, or one measure.
- Confirm that any AI-assisted output was validated.
- Require students to distinguish facts, interpretations, assumptions, and recommendations.
- Use synthetic data only.

## Recommended learning progression

### Beginner

Use cases involving:

- Basic Excel cleaning.
- Simple SQL filtering.
- Aggregation.
- Basic charts.
- Straightforward descriptive KPIs.

### Intermediate

Use cases involving:

- Multiple-table joins.
- Variance analysis.
- DAX measures.
- Root-cause analysis.
- Multi-page dashboards.
- Recommendations.

### Advanced

Use cases involving:

- KPI assurance.
- Security and risk analysis.
- Forecasting.
- Segmentation.
- Cross-departmental decision support.
- Executive reporting.
