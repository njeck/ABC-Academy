# DABA ABC Retail Ltd Integrated Capstone Project

**Document Code:** DABA-CAP-002  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Student Role:** Junior Data Analyst  
**Project Duration:** 3–4 weeks  

## Scenario

ABC Retail Ltd has expanded across several branches and now manages large volumes of sales, customer, employee, inventory, procurement, expense, and budget data. Management believes that performance reporting is inconsistent, inventory shortages are not detected early enough, and important decisions are often made without a consolidated analytical view.

You have been appointed as a junior data analyst to review the available data, identify material issues, perform business analysis, develop a Power BI management dashboard, and present evidence-based recommendations.

## Required business questions

1. Which branches generate the highest and lowest completed sales revenue?
2. Which products generate the most revenue and gross profit?
3. Which customers contribute the most revenue?
4. Which branches and products face inventory shortages?
5. Which departments are overspending against budget?
6. Which sales employees perform best against targets?
7. What are the major patterns in customer complaints?
8. What actions should management prioritize for the next quarter?

## Required deliverables

### 1. Data preparation package

- Original datasets.
- Cleaned datasets.
- Data-quality register.
- Explanation of all corrections.
- Validation evidence.

### 2. Excel analysis

Use Excel for:

- Data profiling.
- Cleaning.
- Lookup and validation.
- Summary calculations.
- Pivot tables.
- Charts where appropriate.

### 3. SQL analysis

Submit:

- A structured SQL query file.
- Queries answering all management questions.
- Comments explaining important query logic.
- Validation checks for totals and joins.

### 4. Power BI report

Create three pages:

1. Executive Overview
2. Sales and Customers
3. Inventory and Finance

Minimum requirements:

- At least 15 DAX measures.
- A marked Date table.
- Correct star-schema relationships.
- Branch, date, department, and product filters.
- Drill-through or tooltip functionality.
- DABA branding.
- Management findings and recommendations.

### 5. AI evidence pack

AI may be used to support:

- Prompt development.
- Formula and query explanation.
- Visual recommendations.
- Insight drafting.
- Management reporting.

Students must submit:

- Original prompts.
- AI outputs.
- Validation records.
- Corrections.
- Final approved conclusions.

### 6. Management report

The written report should contain:

- Executive summary.
- Business context.
- Methodology.
- Data-quality findings.
- Analysis results.
- Dashboard interpretation.
- Five validated findings.
- Three prioritized recommendations.
- One major operational risk.
- One data limitation.
- Conclusion.

### 7. Presentation and defense

Prepare a 10–15 minute presentation followed by panel questions.

You must be able to explain:

- Your cleaning decisions.
- Your SQL queries.
- Your Power BI model.
- Your DAX measures.
- Your AI use.
- The evidence supporting every major recommendation.

All submitted work must be understood and defensible by the student. AI-generated content, formulas, SQL, DAX, or recommendations must be tested and disclosed where required. Fabricated evidence, hidden AI use, or use of real confidential data in an unapproved tool may lead to failure.
