# DABA Capstone Presentation and Defense Rubric

**Document Code:** DABA-CAP-004  
**Maximum Marks:** 25  

| Area | Marks | Assessment Criteria |
|---|---:|---|
| Presentation Structure | 4 | Clear opening, logical flow, appropriate conclusion, professional timing |
| Business Understanding | 4 | Explains the problem, stakeholders, and management questions accurately |
| Technical Explanation | 5 | Clearly explains cleaning, SQL, modeling, DAX, and validation decisions |
| Evidence and Accuracy | 4 | Uses verified figures and connects findings to dashboard or query evidence |
| Recommendations | 3 | Recommendations are practical, prioritized, and supported |
| Responsible AI | 2 | Clearly explains AI use, validation, limitations, and governance |
| Question Handling | 3 | Responds accurately, honestly, and professionally |

## Performance descriptors

### Excellent

The student demonstrates full ownership of the work, explains technical decisions confidently, uses reliable evidence, acknowledges limitations, and answers panel questions directly.

### Competent

The student understands the major components and provides generally accurate explanations, though some answers may lack depth.

### Needs improvement

The student relies heavily on the report or slides, cannot explain important formulas or queries, or presents unsupported recommendations.

### Unsatisfactory

The student cannot demonstrate ownership of the work, provides materially inaccurate answers, or fails to disclose prohibited or unvalidated AI use.
